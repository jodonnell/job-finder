MyLifeRank is a new exciting startup company that will start the next big thing heading into 2011. To understand what MyLifeRank is, here is our executive summary giving a top level overview of what the network is.

MyLifeRank (MLR) is a highly social network that allows you to manage your activities and engage with the activities of your peers and of businesses on the go, while leveraging gaming technology to create you a social rank. The objective is to get users to post their personal goals and accept challenges to improve their rank, which makes the user eligible for better challenges and/or offers from sponsoring businesses. This could include real-time challenges while on the go via mobile. Rank is heavily controlled by a user’s social network’s engagement to the goal or challenge via comments, ratings, etc. to that particular activity (rank relativism) and total rank is made up of several key criteria. Focusing on goals & challenges helps reduce the amount of “noise” around typical social networks that allow for anything and everything to be discussed; MLR is a purpose driven social network.

If you grasp the bigger picture in the summary, then you realize the potential of what we have. There is nothing like that out there.
